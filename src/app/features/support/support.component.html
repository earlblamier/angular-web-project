Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account?

Download the Tazkify app from the App Store and register using your email or supported login options. Identity verification may be required before offering services.

How do I reset my password?

Tap “Forgot Password” on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email. Check your spam folder if you don’t receive the message.

How does booking work?
  • Search for services using location-based features
  • View available skilled individuals
  • Message providers directly
  • Set schedules and confirm service details
How are payments processed?

Payments are processed securely through our integrated payment provider. Tazkify does not store full credit card details on its servers.

How does user verification work?

Users may be required to upload government-issued identification and a profile photo. Ratings and reviews help maintain accountability and trust.

How do ratings and reviews work?

After a completed transaction, users can rate and review their experience. Policy violations may result in account suspension.

What should I do if there is a dispute?

Attempt to resolve concerns respectfully within the app messaging system. If unresolved, contact support with booking details and a clear explanation.
